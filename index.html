Welcome to The Learning Section

Most Essential Learning Competency

Demonstrate an understanding of organisms interacting with each other and with their environment; and the biotic and abiotic components of an ecosystem. After going through this lesson, you are expected to:

  1. Define abiotic and biotic components of ecosystem; and
  2. Identify abiotic and biotic components of ecosystem
